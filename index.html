6affinities



What is 6affinities?

6affinities is, in essence, a philosophical quiz that attempts to assign percentages for six different affinities' values. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be compared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the six affinities?

There are three independent axes - Cultural, Environmental, and Materialistic - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

PURITY

A conservative ideology, believing that humankind must stay pure to itself both in genetics and culture.

VORACITY

An accelerationist ideology, believing that humanity must evolve by any means to adapt to our new world.

HARMONY

An environmentalist ideology, believing that humanity should live in harmony with nature through modification of tradition or genetics with the aim of sustainability.

MASTERY

An environmentalist ideology, believing that humanity should have mastery over their environment. They seek to make nature itself more resilient to make their way of life sustainable.

SUPREMACY

A materialist ideology, believing in the supremacy of the sciences and that technological progress must prioritised above all else. They notion of anything beyond scientific explanation as inherently useless.

DIVINITY

A spiritualist ideology, believing in the divinity of the human mind. Unlike purists they do not have strong feelings about genetics since they consider spiritual and physical bodies separate entities.
